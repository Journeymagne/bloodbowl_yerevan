On a 3+, the selected player is hit. On a 2, the opposition coach selects a player (from either team, but not Zzharg) within 3 squares of the originally selected player to be hit instead. On a 1, Zzharg is hit instead. Make an Armour Roll for whichever player is hit. Zzharg’s activation then immediately ends.
