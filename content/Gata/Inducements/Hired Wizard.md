0-1 Hired Wizard (150k): Once per game, after any player's turn, you may cast one of the following spells:
Fireball: Choose a square; roll a d6 for each player in that and adjacent squares. On a 4+, the player falls; make an armor roll with a +1 modifier.
Toad-Toad: Choose a player and roll a d6. If the result equals or exceeds the player's Strength (6 is always a success), turn them into a toad until the end of the drive. Any injury rolls against the toad are considered Badly Hurt; an apothecary cannot be used. Toad stats: MV 5, ST 1, AG 2+, PA -, AV 5+ (Pogo, Evasive, Dodge, Stunty, No Ball, Titchy, Very Long Legs).
