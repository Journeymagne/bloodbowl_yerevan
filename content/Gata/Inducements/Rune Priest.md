0-1 Rune Priest (50k, only for Old World Classic and Worlds Edge Superleague): At the start of each drive, before the kick, choose one player without the Loner trait and roll a d6: on a 4+, select a rune and apply it to the player (cannot use this effect again for the game); on 2-3, the rune fails but you can repeat in the next drive; on 1, the rune fails and the effect is locked for the game.
Rune of Might: Player gains Mighty Blow until the end of the drive.
Rune of Fury: Player gains Dauntless and Frenzy until the end of the drive.
Rune of Speed: Player gains Sure Feet and Sprint until the end of the drive.
Rune of Iron: Player gains +1 AV and Stand Firm until the end of the drive.
Rune of Force: Player gains Horns and Juggernaut until the end of the drive.
