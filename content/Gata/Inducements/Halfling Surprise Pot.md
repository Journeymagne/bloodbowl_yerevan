0-1 Halfling "Surprise" Pot (80k, or 60k for Halfling): Once per game, after the opponent's turn, choose a square and roll a d6:
6: Pot hits the target.
2-5: Scatter the target square 3 times.
1: d3 players in your reserves move to Knock Out.
The player in the target square falls; make an armor roll with a +2 modifier. Then roll a d6 for each player in adjacent squares; on a 4+, they become Placed Prone.
