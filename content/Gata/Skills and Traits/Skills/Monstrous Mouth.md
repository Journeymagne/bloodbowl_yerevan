## Rule summary
This player may make a Chomp Special Action against a standing opponent they Mark. On 3+, the opponent is Chomped and cannot leave while still Marked by this player. Strip Ball also cannot be used against this player.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Monstrous Mouth can hold no more than one player. If used with Multiple Block, it can hold up to two players.
