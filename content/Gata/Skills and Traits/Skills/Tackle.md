## Rule summary
Opponents Dodging away from this player's tackle zone cannot use Dodge. When this player Blocks, the target cannot count Dodge against Stumble results. In Gata League, Tackle also cancels Evasive.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- When this player Blocks an opponent, the target cannot use Evasive against that Block.
