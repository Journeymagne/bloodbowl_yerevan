## Rule summary
When this player Knocks Down an opponent during a Block Action, they may add +2 to either the Armour roll or Injury roll after seeing the roll.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- When this player applies Mighty Blow (+2), it can fully stack with Claws and Pile Driver. With Claws and Mighty Blow together, any Armour roll of 7+ breaks armour.
