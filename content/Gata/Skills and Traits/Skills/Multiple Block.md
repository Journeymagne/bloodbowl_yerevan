## Rule summary
This player may Block two different adjacent opponents at once, reducing their ST by 2 for the blocks. They cannot Follow-up, and the skill cannot be combined with Frenzy.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- This player may use Multiple Block to affect two adjacent opponents as normal. Multiple Block also works on special actions that replace Block.
