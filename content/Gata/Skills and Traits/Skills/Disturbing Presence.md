## Rule summary
Opponents within 3 squares of this player suffer -1 per such player when passing, throwing a team-mate or bomb, catching, or intercepting.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Disturbing Presence affects ball pickup rolls, but still does not affect Secure the Ball. It does not work while this player is Prone.
