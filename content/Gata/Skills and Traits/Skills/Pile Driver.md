## Rule summary
After this player Knocks Down an opponent during a Block Action, they may make a free Foul Action against that opponent if still standing and Marking them, then this player becomes Prone and their activation ends.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- After an Armour roll from a Block action, this player may become Placed Prone to roll that Armour roll again. This does not work with skills that replace Block. Pile Driver is a Strength skill and fully stacks with Claws and Mighty Blow.
