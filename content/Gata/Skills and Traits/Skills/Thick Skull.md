## Rule summary
This player treats some Knocked-out Injury results as Stunned instead. If they also have Stunty, the adjusted Stunty injury thresholds apply.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Thick Skull is a General skill. This player treats applicable Knocked-out Injury results as Stunned instead.
