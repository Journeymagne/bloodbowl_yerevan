## Rule summary
When this player Knocks Down an opponent during a Block Action, a natural armour roll of 8+ breaks armour regardless of the target's Armour value.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Claws, Mighty Blow, and Pile Driver fully stack. When Claws and Mighty Blow are used together, any Armour roll of 7+ breaks armour.
