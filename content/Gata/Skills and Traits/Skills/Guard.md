## Rule summary
This player may provide Offensive and Defensive Assists for Blocks even while Marked by opposing players.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- During the opponent's turn, this player may provide block assists even while Marked. Guard does not work during your own turn.
