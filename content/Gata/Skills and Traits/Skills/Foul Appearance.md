## Rule summary
An opponent that declares a Block Action or direct Special Action against this player must roll first. On a failed roll, the action is cancelled and the opponent's activation ends.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Foul Appearance triggers on a roll of 1-2 and does not end the acting player's activation.
