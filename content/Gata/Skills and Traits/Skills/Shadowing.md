## Rule summary
When an opponent Dodges out of this player's tackle zone, this player may roll. On 4+ they move into the square the opponent left. Uses per turn are limited by this player's MA.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Shadowing is a General skill. When an opponent dodges out of this player's tackle zone, this player may roll to follow them into the square they left, up to the normal movement limit for Shadowing.
