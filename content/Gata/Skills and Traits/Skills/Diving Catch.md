## Rule summary
This player may try to catch a Pass, Throw-in or Kick-off that lands in their tackle zone, but not a Bounce. If they are in the target square for a Pass Action, they also get +1 to the catch test.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- This player may use Diving Catch for Passes, Throw-ins, Kick-offs, and when Punt is declared.
