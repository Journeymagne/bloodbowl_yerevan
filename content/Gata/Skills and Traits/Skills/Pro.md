## Rule summary
During this player's activation, they may try to re-roll one eligible die, dice pool or die from a multi-dice roll. On 3+ the re-roll is allowed; after trying Pro, the same roll cannot be re-rolled from another source.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)
