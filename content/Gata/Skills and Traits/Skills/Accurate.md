## Rule summary
When this player makes a Quick Pass or Short Pass, they may add +1 to the Passing Ability test.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Accurate and Cannoneer are merged into one skill: Accurate. This player gains +1 to all pass attempts.
