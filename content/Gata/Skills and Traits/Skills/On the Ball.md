## Rule summary
When an opponent declares a Pass Action, this player may move up to 3 squares before the pass roll, without Rushing. One open receiving player with this skill may also move up to 3 squares after kick deviation and before the kick-off event, unless a touchback occurs.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- This player may use On the Ball when an opponent declares a Pass or Punt, and when resolving the normal kick-off movement from this skill.
