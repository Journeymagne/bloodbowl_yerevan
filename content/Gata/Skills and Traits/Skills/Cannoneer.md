## Rule summary
When this player makes a Long Pass or Long Bomb, they may add +1 to the Passing Ability test.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Cannoneer is no longer a separate skill. If a roster, rule, or older reference points to Cannoneer, use Accurate instead: the player gains +1 to all pass attempts.
