## Rule summary
During a Blitz Action, this player may treat Both Down as Push Back on their Block Actions. Targets of those Blitz blocks cannot use Fend, Stand Firm or Wrestle.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- During this player's Blitz blocks, Juggernaut applies as normal and also cancels Foul Appearance.
