## Rule summary
This player ignores negative modifiers when attempting to pick up the ball.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- This player ignores negative modifiers when attempting to pick up the ball. They may also pick up the ball from an adjacent square, but not while in an opponent's control zone.
