## Rule summary
During the opponent's turn, opposing players Marked by this player cannot use Guard or Put the Boot In. In Gata League, Defensive also cancels Offensive.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- During the opponent's turn, opposing players Marked by this player cannot use Guard, Put the Boot In, or Offensive.
