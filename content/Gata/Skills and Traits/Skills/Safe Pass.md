## Rule summary
If this player rolls a natural 1 on a Passing Ability test, the pass is not fumbled. The player keeps the ball, their activation ends, and no Turnover is caused.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Safe Pass protects against any Fumble, not only a natural 1.
