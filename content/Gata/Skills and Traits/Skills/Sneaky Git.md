## Rule summary
When this player Fouls and rolls natural doubles on the Armour roll, they are not Sent-off if the target's armour is not broken. If armour is broken, the normal sending-off rules apply.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- When this player fouls and breaks armour, they are no longer sent off for rolling doubles on the Armour roll.
