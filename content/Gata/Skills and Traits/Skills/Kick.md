## Rule summary
If this player is the nominated kicker, the coach may reduce kick deviation from D6 squares to D3 squares.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- If this player is the nominated kicker, resolve kick deviation by halving the D6 value and rounding down instead of rolling D3. The final result can be 0.
