## Rule summary
Opponents cannot apply Armour-roll modifiers against this player, and Claws cannot be used against them.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Iron Hard Skin grants +1 AV in addition to its basic properties. Its modifier-ignore effect only works during a Block.
