## Rule summary
When this player is Pushed Back by a Block Action, this player's coach may force the opponent to Follow-up. It cannot force a Rooted Take Root player to move.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)
