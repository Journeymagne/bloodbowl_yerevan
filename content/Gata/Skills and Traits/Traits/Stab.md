## Rule summary
This player may make a Stab Special Action against an adjacent standing opponent. Make an unmodified Armour roll; if armour breaks, roll Injury. It can replace the Block in a Blitz, but then the activation ends.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- This player may use Stab as a special action against an adjacent standing opponent. During a Blitz, Stab may replace the Block action and no longer ends this player's movement.
