## Rule summary
This player may perform the Throw Team-mate Action. Use the normal Throw Team-mate procedure for picking up and throwing an eligible team-mate.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- Throw Team-Mate is a passive skill. This player may perform Throw Team-Mate using the normal throwing procedure for an eligible team-mate.
