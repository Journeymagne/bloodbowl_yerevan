## Rule summary
This player can only take a Ball and Chain Special Action. They move randomly with the throw-in template, automatically dodge away, crash into players, push prone players, bounce the ball, and risk Injury by the Crowd if they leave the pitch.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)
