## Rule summary
This player may make a Hypnotic Gaze Special Action after moving. Choose an adjacent standing opponent; on a successful roll that opponent becomes Distracted and this player's activation ends.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- Once per turn, this player may use Hypnotic Gaze as a special action against an adjacent standing opponent. Resolve the gaze as normal; on success, the target becomes Distracted. No team may use Hypnotic Gaze more than once in the same turn.
