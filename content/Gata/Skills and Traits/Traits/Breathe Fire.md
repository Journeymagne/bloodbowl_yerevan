## Rule summary
This player may make a Breathe Fire Special Action against a standing adjacent opponent. Roll D6 with -1 against ST 5+ targets; high results place the target Prone or Knocked Down, and the action ends the activation.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- This player may use Breathe Fire as a special action against an adjacent standing opponent. A roll of 1 does not cause Knocked Down.
