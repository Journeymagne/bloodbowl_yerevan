## Rule summary
Once per game, if this player kills an eligible opposing player with a Block Action and the death is not saved, add a new Lineman from this team roster to Reserves. It may be hired after the game like a Journeyman.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- When a player with Plague Ridden or Masters of Undeath kills an opponent, their coach gains a Lineman from their own team. Shambling Undead may choose either a Skeleton or a Zombie.
