## Rule summary
During movement, this player may Pogo over one adjacent square like a Jump while ignoring the usual negative jump modifiers. This trait cannot be combined with Leap.

Full wording: https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/

## Gata League change
- During this player's movement, they may use Pogo to jump over one adjacent square. Pogo can only be used once per activation.
