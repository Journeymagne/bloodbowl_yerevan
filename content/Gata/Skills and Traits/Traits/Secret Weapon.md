## Rule summary
At the end of any drive in which this player took part, they are Sent-off, even if they are no longer on the pitch when the drive ends.

Full wording: [Blood Bowl 2025 skills and traits reference](https://bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/)

## Gata League change
- At the end of a drive, Secret Weapon only applies if this player was fielded for that drive.
