# 5. Patch Notes

Season 2 Patch Notes

General

Many minor unlisted changes to player costs; please check.

OTD (One-Turn Touchdown) is permitted when using the Throw Teammate skill.

Friendly match limit: 3 per month.

Maximum team size increased to 14.

Secure the Ball! range is now 1 square.

Added "Coach's Safe" rule: After each game, you may send up to 50k in winnings to a safe for a "rainy day," as well as any number of league winnings. These cannot be spent until the end of the season; however, after a redraft, you may add up to 300k from these reserves when building any team.

Bribery and Corruption allows purchasing up to 3 Bribes per team; they increase Team Value (TV).

The costs of many players with 8+ armor have been recalculated; please check your team list.

The Weather Change event now checks weather on 1d6. The weather table has been adapted accordingly.

Passing Virtuosos: No longer grants extra experience for passing. Instead, the receiver of a successful Accurate pass also receives 1 SPP.

Any external armor reduction sources (mages, stadiums, etc.) are applied after skill-based armor reduction and cannot reduce the final armor value below 7+.

Fully painted teams receive an additional 30k gold after a match.

All Team Rerolls now cost 120k gold; they can be bought for 60k gold during team drafts as usual.

Captain rule changed: It grants the Captain player Pro. You must field them if able, and while they are on the field, roll 5+ when using a Reroll; on success, it is not consumed.

Block now only works if its owner declares a Block (e.g., if you are being blocked, Block does not work). The cost of some players with Block has been revised.

Dodge is split into Dodge and Evasive. Its effects are divided: Dodge allows rerolling leaving Tackle Zones; Evasive converts Stumble to Push Back. Many players lost one of these two skills.

For the first 3 games after creation, all injuries inflicted on team players are considered Badly Hurt.

Favoured Of...: Now rolls on a d8. Slaanesh triggers on 6, Nurgle on 7, Khorne on 8, Tzeentch on 9(6), Hashut on 4.

Shambling Undead and Necromantic: Zombie Lineman access to 'D' is now secondary.

Chaos Dwarf: Hobgoblin Lineman now costs 40k. Chaos Dwarf Flamesmith lost Brawler and now costs 80k. Chaos Dwarf Blocker costs 65k. Bull Centaur gained Leap and Horns; now costs 150k. Players with the Dwarf tag lost access to 'A' (short legs, excluding Bull Centaur, as they have legs from a different creature). Team moved to Tier 1.

Vampire: Thrall Lineman now costs 40k. Vampire Thrower now costs 115k. Apothecaries are banned.

Skills

Armbar forbids any dodge rerolls.

Shadowing is now a General skill.

Frenzy is now optional; the skill is declared immediately before the first block action in an activation.

Throw Teammate is now a passive skill.

Monstrous Mouth: Allows holding no more than 1 player. Exception: if used with Multiple Block, then up to 2 can be held.

Big Hand: In addition to old wording, now allows picking up the ball while in an adjacent square (but not while in an opponent's control zone).

Disturbing Presence: Works on ball pickup (still does not affect Secure the Ball). Does not work when the player is prone.

Foul Appearance: Now triggers on a 1-2 and does not end the player's activation upon triggering.

Iron Hard Skin: Now grants +1 to AV in addition to basic properties.

Tentacles: Now simply works on a raw roll of 4+ regardless of model strength.

New Mutation - Bone Hook: Before performing a foul action, you may move the target player to 1 square adjacent to the active player (occurs before calculating assists).

Leap: Now always works on an unmodifiable 4+ roll (improved by Very Long Legs mutation).

New General Skill - Elvenball: Allows movement after a Secure the Ball action.

Guard: Now only works during the opponent's turn.

Offensive (New General Skill): Grants an unblockable assist for a block during your turn.

Defensive: Now cancels the Offensive skill (functionally unchanged).

Stab: Removed from the skill list; it is now a trait, as it was before.

Shiv (New Skill): Works like Stab, but can only be used once per turn among all players with Shiv.

Bloodlust: New wording. Immediately after declaring any action by a player with this rule, roll a d6: on a 2+, the turn continues normally. If a 1 is rolled, the player must go "have a snack." The action is lost, and the player may choose a teammate without the Vampire or Undead tags in an adjacent square to roll for injury against, treating any result on the injury table as Badly Hurt. This roll causes a Turnover only if the Thrall was holding the ball. Before the bite, the player may declare movement. After the bite, the player's activation ends. If the bite is not performed, a Turnover occurs.

Breathe Fire: No longer causes Knocked Down on a roll of 1.

Brawler: Cannot be used in negative dice blocks.

Elite Skills: Deleted. Instead, the concept of "Elite Combos" is added. Having one on a player adds +15k to their cost. The following combos are elite: Claws+Mighty Blow, Guard+Defensive, Wrestle+Evasive, Sneaky Git+Dirty Player.

Diving Catch and On the Ball now work when Punt is declared.

Kick: Changed. Instead of a d6 roll, halve the d6 value during kickoff, rounding down. The final value can be 0.

Leader: Triggers at the start of a drive.
